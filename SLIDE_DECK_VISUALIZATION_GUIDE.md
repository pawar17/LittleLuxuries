# Slide Deck Visualization Guide
## Which Visualizations to Use with Each Slide

---

## Slide 5: Key Finding #1 - Search Behavior
**Use:** `Viz/search_indicators_ranking.png`
- Shows all 8 indicators ranked by R²
- Highlights significant vs. non-significant indicators
- Perfect visual for the ranking table

---

## Slide 6: Search Behavior - What This Means
**Use:** `Viz/search_indicators_ranking.png` (same as Slide 5)
- Or create a simple bar chart showing top 4 indicators
- Can also use `Viz/temporal_trends.png` to show time series

---

## Slide 7: Key Finding #2 - Census Data
**Use:** `Viz/purchase_behavior_analysis.png`
- Shows purchase behavior patterns
- Can also create a custom chart showing the R² values for Census data

---

## Slide 8: Recession Analysis
**Use:** `Viz/temporal_trends.png`
- Shows time series trends
- Can highlight recession periods
- Or create a custom bar chart showing recession period changes

---

## Slide 9: Retail Transaction Analysis
**Use:** `Viz/purchase_behavior_analysis.png`
- Shows category breakdown
- Price point analysis
- Luxury spending patterns

---

## Slide 10: Search vs Purchase Comparison
**Use:** `Viz/search_vs_purchase_comparison.png`
- Perfect visual for this slide
- Shows the difference between search and purchase behavior
- Highlights the key insight

---

## Slide 11: The Complete Picture
**Use:** `Viz/simplified_lipstick_effect.png` (if available)
- Or create a flowchart/diagram showing the two-stage process
- Can combine elements from multiple visualizations

---

## Slide 13: Visualizations
**Use:** All 4 main visualizations:
1. `Viz/search_indicators_ranking.png`
2. `Viz/temporal_trends.png`
3. `Viz/purchase_behavior_analysis.png`
4. `Viz/search_vs_purchase_comparison.png`

---

## Quick Reference: Visualization Files

| Visualization File | Best For | Slide Numbers |
|-------------------|----------|---------------|
| `search_indicators_ranking.png` | Search behavior results, indicator rankings | 5, 6, 13 |
| `temporal_trends.png` | Time series, recession periods, trends over time | 6, 8, 13 |
| `purchase_behavior_analysis.png` | Purchase patterns, categories, price points | 7, 9, 13 |
| `search_vs_purchase_comparison.png` | Comparing search vs purchase behavior | 10, 13 |
| `simplified_lipstick_effect.png` | Overall concept, complete picture | 11 |

---

## Tips for Presentation

1. **Slide 5-6:** Use the ranking chart to show which indicators are significant
2. **Slide 7-8:** Use time series to show Census data trends and recession periods
3. **Slide 10:** The comparison chart is perfect for showing search ≠ purchase
4. **Slide 13:** Showcase all visualizations to demonstrate comprehensive analysis

---

## Creating Custom Slides

If you need additional visualizations:
- **Recession period chart:** Bar chart showing beauty sales changes during each recession
- **R² comparison chart:** Side-by-side comparison of search vs. Census R² values
- **Timeline:** Visual timeline showing data coverage (2004-2024 for search, 1992-2025 for Census)
